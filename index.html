About Disney

What is Disney? Is it The Lion King? Beauty and the Beast? Is it Mulan's bravery? Or Tinkerbell's sparkle? Disney is the magic that we grew up with, the lessons we learned, and all the adventures we had, as children. Life may carry on, life may get busy, life may get tough, and we get older, but the Disney magic never fades away. As Disney celebrates 100 years, we want people to remember the same Disney magic, the same feeling when we were kids, 20 years later.

It all starts, with a little pixie dust!
